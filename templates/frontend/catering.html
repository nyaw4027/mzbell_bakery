Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I order?

We recommend ordering at least 72 hours in advance for standard items. For large events or custom items, please allow 1-2 weeks.

Do you offer tastings?

Yes! We offer tastings for orders over $300. Schedule an appointment to sample our menu items.

What's your cancellation policy?

Orders can be cancelled up to 48 hours before the event for a full refund. Later cancellations may incur charges.

Do you provide serving utensils?

Yes, we provide all necessary serving utensils, plates, and napkins with our catering packages.

Can you accommodate allergies?

Absolutely! Please inform us of any allergies when ordering. We take special precautions to prevent cross-contamination.

What's your delivery area?

We deliver within 25 miles of our bakery. Additional delivery fees may apply for longer distances.
